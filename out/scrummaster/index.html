Course Syllabus

Scrum Master

Scrum Master Syllabus

  • 1. Introduction to Agile & Scrum
    • Understanding Agile Manifesto & its principles
    • Scrum Framework: Roles, Artifacts, Ceremonies, Values.
  • 2. Scrum Master Framework: ROLE
    • Responsibilities:
    • Scrum Master
    • Product Owner
    • Development Team
  • 3. Scrum Master Framework: VALUE
    • Scrum is Guided by Five core Values
    • Commitment
    • Courage
    • Focus
    • Openness
    • Respect
  • 4. Scrum Master Framework: ARTIFACT
    • Three Primary Artifacts in Scrum:
    • Product Backlog
    • Sprint Backlog
    • Increment
  • 5. Scrum Master Framework: EVENTS(CEREMONIES):
    • Four Main Events:
    • Sprint and its Planning
    • Daily Scrum
    • Sprint Review
    • Sprint Retrospective
  • 6. Facilitation And Coaching:
    • Techniques for Facilitating Scrum Events:
    • Create a Structured Agenda
    • Set Time-Boxes
    • Encourage Participation
    • Use Visual Aids
    • Facilitate Decision Making
    • Manage Conflicts
    • Coaching the Scrum Team for Self-Organization & Improvement:
    • Encourage Ownership
    • Promote Continuous Improvement
    • Provide Feedback
    • Foster a Learning Culture
    • Handling Conflict within the Scrum Team:
    • Address Issues Promptly
    • Listen Actively
    • Facilitate Dialogue
    • Mediate if Necessary
    • Follow Up
  • 7. Agile Estimation & Planning
    • User Stories
    • Product Backlog Items
    • Creating & Managing the Sprint backlogs:
    • Sprint Planning
    • Task Decomposition
    • Estimating Tasks
    • Updating the Sprint Backlogs
  • 8. Agile Metrics & Reporting
    • Agile Metrics:
    • Velocity
    • Burndown & Burnup Chart
    • Lead Time & Cycle Time
    • Cumulative Flow Diagram
    • Escaped Defects
    • Customer Satisfaction
    • Work in Progress
    • Agile Reporting:
    • Transparency & Visibility
    • Frequency & Cadence
    • Focus on Values & Outcomes
    • Visualization & Dashboards
    • Key Metrics & Trends
    • Impediments & Risks
    • Continuous Improvement
    • Actionable Insights
    • Adaptability & Flexibility
  • 9. Agile and Scrum Software
    • Jira
    • Trello
    • Confluence
    • How to use the tools efficiently in Scrum Projects:
    • Backlog Management
    • Task Tracking & Process Monitoring
    • Daily Stand-ups
  • 10. Scaling Scrum
    • Introduction to Scaling Framework:
    • SAFe (Scaled Agile Framework)
    • LeSS (Large-Scale Scrum)
    • Scrum@Scale
    • Consideration For Scaling Scrum in Larger Organizations:
    • Organizational Structure
    • Culture & Mindset
    • Training & Coaching
    • Communication & Collaboration
    • Incremental Adoption
    • Feedback &Adaptation
  • 11. Agile Transformation & Culture
    • Strategies For Introducing Scrum & Agile Principles:
    • Education & Training
    • Pilot Projects
    • Executive Support
    • Cross- Functional Collaboration
    • Adaptive Planning & Iterative Delivery
    • Strategies for Building an Agile Culture & Mindset:
    • Lead by Example
    • Empowerment & Autonomy
    • Customer Focus
    • Embrace Failure & Learn from Mistakes
  • 12. Continuous Learning & Improvement
    • Encouraging a Culture of Continuous Improvement:
    • Promote Psychological Safety
    • Empower Teams
    • Celebrate Successes & Failures
    • Implement Feedback Mechanisms
    • Staying Updated on the Latest Trends & Best Practices for Agile & Scrum
  • 13. Scrum Master Ethics & Professionalism
    • Ethical Consideration in the Scrum Master Role:
    • Confidentiality, Impartiality, Transparency
    • Conflict of Interest, Respect for Autonomy
    • Honesty & Integrity
    • Maintaining Professionalism & Integrity:
    • Leading by Example
    • Effective Communication, Conflict Resolution, Accountability
    • Ethical Decision-Making
  • 14. Case Studies, Real-World Scenarios, Practice Exercises
    • Analyzing Real-World scrum implementation challenges & Solutions
    • Learning from Success & Failure Stories
    • Hands-on practice projects
  • 15. Scrum Master Certification (Optional)
    • Certified Scrum Master (CSM)
    • Professional Scrum Master (PSM)
    • SAFe Scrum Master (SSM)
    • Certified Agile Coach (CAC)
    • Agile Certified Practitioner (PMI-ACP)

Frequently AskedQuestions

1. What's the buzz about Scrum?

Scrum isn't just another project management frameworkit's like the secret sauce that transforms chaotic projects into well-oiled machines. Think of it as your ticket to smoother workflows, happier teams, and impressive results!

2. So, what exactly does a Scrum Master do? Are they like project management superheroes?

You bet they are! Scrum Masters are the unsung heroes of Agile teams. They swoop in, break down barriers, and keep the project train chugging along. Plus, they're the ultimate team cheerleaders, making sure everyone feels heard, valued, and ready to tackle any challenge.

3. What cool stuff will I learn in this Scrum Master course?

Get ready for a rollercoaster ride of Agile awesomeness! We'll dive deep into Agile and Scrum fundamentals, teach you the Jedi ways of Scrum Masters, and equip you with supercharged collaboration and problem-solving skills.

4. Can a total newbie like me join this course, or do I need some training first?

Whether you're a fresh-faced newbie or a seasoned pro looking to level up, this course has something for everyone. We'll start from square one and guide you through the magical world of Agile and Scrum at your own pace.

5. Are there any prerequisites for unleashing my inner Scrum Master?

Nope, zero prerequisites requiredjust bring your enthusiasm and a thirst for knowledge! If you've dabbled in project management or have a knack for teamwork, you're already ahead of the game.

6. What is the duration and format of the course, and how can participants enrol?

Our course offerings are online instructor-led sessions and interactive workshops. Participants can choose the option that best suits their schedule and learning preferences. Enrolment is simple and can be completed online through our website.

7. Upon completion of the course, will participants receive a certification?

Yes, participants who successfully complete the course requirements will receive a Course Completion Certificate.

8. How will this course turbocharge my career?

You will be leading teams with confidence, navigating complex projects like a pro, and earning the admiration of colleagues and bosses alike. With a Scrum Master certification under your belt, the sky's the limit for your career!

9. Will I have the opportunity to engage with Agile experts during the course?

Certainly! Our seasoned team of Agile professionals is here to provide guidance, share insights, and address any inquiries you may have. Think of them as your dedicated mentors throughout this learning journey.

10. Sounds awesome! Where do I sign up for this magical journey?

Ready to embark on the adventure of a lifetime? Head over to our website, grab your cape (figuratively speaking), and hit that enrol button! Your Scrum Master destiny awaits!
